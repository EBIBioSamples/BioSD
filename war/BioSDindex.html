The BioSample Database is a new database at EBI designed to hold information about biological samples used in experiments. The samples will be issued stable accession numbers, which later can be referenced from submissions to other EBI databases, such as ArrayExpress, PRIDE and ENA. We are working to develop a joint biosample accessioning system with our colleagues at NCBI letter
